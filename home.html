Kampala Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Kampala Chamber of Commerce

The name represents an organization dedicated to supporting businesses, promoting economic growth, and connecting entrepreneurs throughout Kampala, Uganda.

Site Purpose

The Kampala Chamber of Commerce website serves as a resource hub for businesses and residents. It provides business directories, networking opportunities, community events, membership information, and resources that encourage economic development.

Target Market

  • Small business owners
  • Corporate organizations
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Local residents
  • Investors
  • Visitors to Kampala

Site Goals

  • Promote local businesses.
  • Increase chamber membership.
  • Provide networking opportunities.
  • Support economic development.
  • Encourage local shopping.

User Personas

Small Business Owner

Sarah owns a clothing boutique and wants networking opportunities, marketing support, and business resources.

Corporate Executive

David represents a large company seeking partnerships, investment opportunities, and economic development information.

New Resident

Brian recently moved to Kampala and wants to discover local businesses, services, and community events.

Scenarios

  • A business owner wants to learn about chamber membership and benefits.
  • A resident searches for upcoming networking events and workshops.
  • An investor explores Kampala's business environment before opening a company.

SEO Plan

  • Use descriptive page titles and meta descriptions.
  • Include relevant business keywords.
  • Register with Google Business Profile.
  • Use Google Analytics.
  • Encourage backlinks from member businesses.

Design Brief
